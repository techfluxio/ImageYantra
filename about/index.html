🚀 About ImageYantra

Free, fast, and secure image tools — all inside your browser.

💡 What is ImageYantra?

ImageYantra is a free online toolkit that allows you to convert images to PDF, resize images, compress files, and change resolution — all directly in your browser.

⚡ Why ImageYantra?

Unlike many other tools, ImageYantra does not upload your files to any server. Everything happens locally on your device, ensuring speed and privacy.

🔒 Privacy First

Your data is never stored or shared. All processing is done securely inside your browser.

🎯 Our Mission

To provide simple, powerful, and free tools that anyone can use without signup, payment, or technical knowledge.
