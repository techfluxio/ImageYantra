Why Convert Images to PDF?

PDF is the universal document format. Whether you're submitting scanned paperwork, combining product photos for a client, or archiving a photo album, converting to PDF ensures consistent appearance across every device and operating system.

A PDF also lets you combine multiple images into a single, organized document — something that's difficult to do with image files alone.

Step-by-Step: Convert Images to PDF with ImageYantra

  1. Open the tool. Go to the Image to PDF converter on ImageYantra.
  2. Add your images. Click the drop zone or drag your JPG, PNG, or WEBP files in. You can add multiple images — each becomes a separate page.
  3. Reorder if needed. Drag the image thumbnails to set the page order.
  4. Choose page settings. Select A4, Letter, or "fit to image" and portrait or landscape orientation.
  5. Generate and download. Click "Generate PDF" and download your file instantly.

Choosing the Right Page Size

A4 is the standard in most of the world and works well for documents. Letter (8.5 × 11 in) is common in North America. Fit to image preserves the original image dimensions exactly — ideal when you don't want any white borders or cropping.

Tips for Best Results

  • Use PNG for documents with text — the lossless format keeps text sharp.
  • For photographs, WEBP or JPG produce smaller PDFs.
  • Set orientation to landscape for wide images like spreadsheet screenshots.
  • All processing is local — your images are never uploaded anywhere.

Privacy & Security

ImageYantra uses the jsPDF library to generate PDFs entirely in your browser. No image data is transmitted over the network. This makes the tool safe for confidential documents like contracts, medical records, and financial statements.

Your files stay on your device. ImageYantra never uploads your images to any server. Everything happens in your browser using the Canvas API and jsPDF.
