Guide

How to Crop Images Online — Tips & Techniques

Cropping removes the outer parts of an image to highlight a subject, remove distractions, or fit a specific frame. Unlike resizing, cropping always reduces the number of pixels — making it faster and lossless within what remains.

Pro Tips for Better Crops

  • Use the Rule of Thirds — place your subject at the intersection of the grid lines, not dead center
  • Leave breathing room above the subject's head in portrait crops
  • For product shots, crop tight — minimal background draws focus to the product
  • When cropping for social, always check the platform's required ratio first
