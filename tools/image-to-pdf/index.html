How to Convert Images to PDF — Complete Guide

Converting images to PDF creates a document that's universally viewable, printable, and shareable. PDFs maintain image quality, page layout, and aspect ratio across all devices and operating systems — making them ideal for sharing photos professionally.

Why Convert Images to PDF?

  • Share multiple photos as a single organized document
  • Submit photos for forms and applications that require PDF format
  • Create a photo album or portfolio in a universally viewable format
  • Preserve scanned documents with images embedded in PDF structure
  • Combine different image formats (JPG + PNG + WEBP) into one PDF

Page Size Options

The tool supports A4, Letter, and image-fit (the PDF page size matches your image dimensions exactly). For most official document submissions, A4 or Letter is recommended. For photo portfolios, image-fit preserves the original aspect ratio perfectly.
