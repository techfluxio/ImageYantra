Guide

How to Remove Image Backgrounds with AI

This tool uses the RMBG-1.4 AI model running entirely in your browser via WebAssembly. It detects the foreground subject — people, products, animals — and removes only the background, leaving the subject perfectly intact with clean, smooth edges. No account, no API key, no upload required.

Common Use Cases

  • Product photography — isolate items against transparent or white backgrounds for e-commerce
  • Profile photos — remove busy backgrounds for professional headshots
  • Graphic design — extract subjects for compositing in Canva, Photoshop, or Figma
  • ID photos and documents requiring plain-background portraits
  • Social media stickers and custom PNG cutouts

Privacy & How It Works

Unlike cloud-based tools, ImageYantra's background removal runs entirely on your device using WebAssembly. The AI model is downloaded once, cached in your browser, then processes every image locally — no image data is ever sent to any server.
